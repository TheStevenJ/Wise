How to Help Save our Planet

There is many ways you can help and reduce the waste of plastic and metal cans and trash around the world by practicing recylcing at home or at school and see what items can be used even after their use is done.

Here Are Some Tips That You Can Use To Help Save Our Home

Tip #1

To help your recycling center and to keep down foul odors, make sure you wash food waste off of any plastic or glass food containers before putting them in the recycle bin.

Tip #2

It is not just newspapers that you can recycle at home. Also, recycle wrapping paper, envelopes, birthday cards and phone books. All sorts of cardboard can be recycled, too, even toilet and paper towel tubes.

Tip #5

Begin a composting program in your school cafeteria to reduce the amount of food waste thrown away. Some cafeterias can use waste compactors that will reduce how much space kitchen and food waste occupies.

Tip #6

Think about making schools as paperless as possible. Students and teachers can do a lot of their work with email, electronic documents and online rather than printing out many paper copies for students. Staff and administration for the school also should understand how to make minor changes that can make more of the school curriculum paperless.
